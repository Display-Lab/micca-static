Data upload and reporting cycles follow this schedule:

Q1: January, February, and March

  • Data uploaded: 2nd full week of June
  • Reports generated: 4th full week of June

Q2: April, May, and June

  • Data uploaded: 2nd full week of September
  • Reports generated: 4th full week of September

Q3: July, August, and September

  • Data uploaded: 2nd full week of December
  • Reports generated: 4th full week of December

Q4: October, November, and December

  • Data uploaded: 2nd full week of March
  • Reports generated: 4th full week of March
