Michigan Collaborative for Contraceptive Access(MICCA)

M5 Counseling

Measure Name: Contraceptive Counseling

 

Measure Description

Percentage of women aged 15-44 years who had a live birth and were provided postpartum contraception counseling

 

Rationale

Counseling women about the range of contraceptive options will help most women to understand the most or moderately effective methods available and could help them chose.

Care Setting

Primary Care and Reproductive Health Settings

 

Scope

Measured on a per woman basis

Data Collection Method

This measure shall be calculated on the basis of data collected from the EMR/Medical Record.

Measure Frequency

This measure is to be reported for every quarter of the measuring year.

Q1        January, February, and March

Q2        April, May, and June

Q3        July, August, and September

Q4        October, November, and December

 

Measure Calculation

Ratio-based

Numerator Statement

Women aged 15-44 years who had a live birth during the measuring quarter of the year and were provided postpartum contraception counseling

 

Denominator Statement

Women aged 15-44 years who had a live birth during the measuring quarter of the year.

Allowed Values

  • Yes
  • No
  • Unknown

Inclusions

Women aged 15-44 years who had a live birth within the hospital setting in that measuring quarter of the year

 

Exclusions:

Women who did not have a live birth(i.e., miscarriage, ectopic, stillbirth or induced abortion) in that measuring quarter of the year

 

Risk Adjustment (for outcome measures)

No risk adjustment or risk stratification
